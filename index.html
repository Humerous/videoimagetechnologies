Our Director

Meet Our Visionary Director: David Miller

David Miller brings over 13 years of industry expertise to Video Image Technologies. Starting at Scarab Industries, he excels as a Digital Imaging Technician (DIT) and Q-Take Operator, collaborating with leaders like Anthony Dod Mantle.

David's global experience includes working with Japanese Director Hideyuki Tanaka. With a sound engineering diploma, he seamlessly integrates video and sound for top clients like Coca Cola, Sony, and Toyota. David's aim is to grow Video Image Technologies into a distinguished brand, driven by innovation and excellence.

His extensive experience and creativity position him as a leader in digital media production. Choose David Miller and Video Image Technologies for reliable and experienced professional services in the film and media industry.
